## 2. Expected Bug Semantics

The JTAG block exports `jtag_unlock`. This signal is connected to `reglk_wrapper`, where it is included in the reset condition. When `jtag_unlock` is asserted, `reglk_mem` is reset to zero, disabling register locks globally.

## 4. Hit Criteria

- Cites `jtag_unlock` in the `reglk_wrapper` reset condition.
- Explains impact on global register-lock state.
