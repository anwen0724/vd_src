| official_description | DMA transfer does not check for the max length condition in syscall |
| task_category | DMA/syscall bounds |

## 2. Expected Bug Semantics

DMA syscall wrappers pass `length-1` directly through syscall argument `a2` into the DMA programming path, while the DMA RTL assumes a bounded transfer length and uses `length_d` to drive PMP checking without enforcing the documented max-length condition.
